The Agro-Tech Scenario

In durian plantation technology (e.g., managing prized Musang King D197 crops), monitoring development stages is crucial. Agronomists cannot just measure size; they must understand the instantaneous growth rate ($f'(x)$) to dynamically schedule irrigation, potassium-rich fertilization, and harvest planning.

Bioscience Insight:

Using standard Sigmoid curve kinetics published by MARDI & the Malaysian Dept. of Agriculture, we model the physical fruit weight expansion over 120 Days After Anthesis (DAA).

The Mathematical Model

Logistic Growth Function, \(f(x)\)
$$f(x) = \frac{L}{1 + e^{-k(x - x_0)}}$$

Calculates the cumulative fruit mass (grams) at day \(x\).

Instantaneous Rate, \(f'(x)\)
$$f'(x) = \frac{L \cdot k \cdot e^{-k(x - x_0)}}{(1 + e^{-k(x - x_0)})^2}$$

Calculates the instantaneous growth rate (grams per day) using the chain rule.

\(L = 2000\text{g}\) Max Capacity
\(k = 0.08\) Growth Factor
\(x_0 = 60\text{d}\) Inflection Point
